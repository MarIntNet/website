About marint.net

The Network

Marint.net is a network for researchers and others working with machine learning technologies in the context of marine and aquatic science and management. The network is centered around a grant proposal to EU's COST program, which we hope will finance important activities including networking and knowledge exchange, building important capability to leverage these tools for the benefit of our oceans and freshwater resources. If you are interested in becoming a part of the network, please send me an email at ketil@malde.org.

Participating institutions

Institute of Marine Research, Bergen, Norway.
